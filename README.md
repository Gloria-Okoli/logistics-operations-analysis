# 🚛 Driver Performance Intelligence Dashboard
### Jan 2022 - Dec 2024

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing driver performance,
incident rates, fuel efficiency, and revenue metrics across a
logistics company fleet — built as part of an ongoing logistics
operations dataset analysis.

---

## 📌 Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of driver performance
intelligence across a 3-year period (2022–2024), covering 124 active
drivers. It enables logistics managers to monitor on-time delivery
rates, identify top performers, track fuel costs, and analyze
incident patterns by experience group.

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop** — Interactive dashboards and visual reports
- **DAX** — Calculated measures and KPI metrics
- **Data Modeling** — Relationships across large logistics dataset
- **Power Query** — Data cleaning and transformation
- **Slicers & Filters** — Dynamic filtering by driver, terminal,
  employment status, and year

---

## 📊 What's Inside

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **KPI Cards** | Active Drivers, Average MPG, On-Time Rate, Total Incidents, Revenue Per Driver |
| **Top Drivers by OTD Rate** | Ranked on-time delivery performance per driver |
| **Incident Rate by Experience** | Incident analysis segmented by years of experience |
| **Total Revenue by Driver** | Individual driver revenue contribution comparison |
| **Average Idle Hours Trend** | Monthly idle hours trend across Jan–Dec |
| **Driver Count by Experience Group** | Distribution of drivers across experience bands |
| **Fuel Cost Per Mile by Driver** | Individual fuel efficiency and cost tracking |
| **Dynamic Slicers** | Filter by Driver Name, Home Terminal, Employment Status, Year |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Fleet of **124 active drivers** tracked across Jan 2022 – Dec 2024
- Overall **On-Time Delivery Rate: 55.68%** across all regions
- **Average fuel efficiency: 6.50 MPG** across the fleet
- **169 total incidents** recorded over the 3-year period
- **Patricia Martin** led all drivers in OTD rate at **56.60%**
- Drivers with **11–15 years experience** had the highest incident
  count at 63 — flagging a need for targeted safety review
- **Thomas Gomez** generated the highest individual revenue at **$5.2M**
- **Karen Lopez** recorded the highest fuel cost per mile at **$0.8442**
- Average idle hours peaked in **May at 2,424.1 hours**
- **20+ year experience** drivers made up the largest group at 25.33%
